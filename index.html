The Lines in Between

Poetry is one of the most beautiful forms of art.Be it embedded in recent
or ancient literature,spoken in events or shows it will always give a magnificent aura.
You really wouldn't want to miss out.

Cliche' but still Authentic

I fell in love with the
way you fall asleep,slowly
and then all at once

Everything will be alright
in the end, so if it is not
alright, it is not the end

Roses are red, blue berries
are blue,God made me pretty
what happened to you

We are writers my love
we don't cry we bleed
on paper

I don't think we meet people by mistake.
People are lessons, vessels, connections.
I don't think we met by mistake.To
me you were all of those things.
You taught me what love is and how it feels to be loved deeply. 

~Anonymous
